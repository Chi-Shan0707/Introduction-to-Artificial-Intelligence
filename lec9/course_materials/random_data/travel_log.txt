Travel Log - Tokyo Trip
========================

Day 1: Arrived at Narita Airport. Took the Narita Express to Shinjuku.
Checked into the hotel and walked around Kabukicho at night.

Day 2: Visited Senso-ji temple in Asakusa. Had amazing ramen at a
small shop near the station. Explored Akihabara in the afternoon.

Day 3: Day trip to Kamakura. Saw the Great Buddha statue.
The hiking trail between temples was beautiful.

Day 4: Tsukiji outer market for breakfast sushi. Shibuya crossing
and Harajuku in the afternoon. Best crepe I've ever had.

Overall budget: ¥150,000 (~$1,000 USD)
